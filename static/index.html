🌸 Insomnia

Insomnia involves persistent difficulty falling or staying asleep, causing daytime fatigue and impaired cognitive function.

  • Difficulty falling asleep at night
  • Waking during the night frequently
  • Daytime tiredness or low energy
  • Irritability, anxiety, or low mood
  • Difficulty concentrating on tasks

🔴 Sleep Apnea

Sleep apnea involves repeated breathing interruptions during sleep, reducing oxygen and causing poor rest quality.

  • Loud snoring or gasping during sleep
  • Morning headaches or dry mouth
  • Excessive daytime drowsiness
  • High blood pressure often linked to apnea
  • Please consult a physician for a sleep study
